Appliora – Your Job Application Tracker

Appliora helps you track your job applications and keep them organized in one place.

What You Can Do

1. Add a New Application

- Click “+ Add Application” at the top.

- Fill in the company name, position, job type, status, date, resume, and any notes.

- If the status is Rejected, you can add a rejection reason.

- Click Add Application to save it.

2. View Your Applications

- See a dashboard with Total Applications, Interviews, Rejected, and Other.

- Applications appear in a list at the bottom once added.

3. Search Applications

- Use the search bar at the top to quickly find a job by name.

4. Manage Your Applications

- You can see all added applications in the list.

5. Contact the Developer

- Click “Contact me” at the bottom

- Fill out your name, email, subject, and message to send a query or feedback.

6. Sign Out

- Click Sign Out to exit the app.
